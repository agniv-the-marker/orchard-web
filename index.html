`home`

[orchard] is a home for innovators, artists, writers, and engineers to make something new alongside people who care and inspire.

{ sessions }

we host ~3 hour sessions to work on projects and meet other makers on tuesday nights and saturday mornings; check out our calendar for info and signup for upcoming events.
