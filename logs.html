Jan 4th, 2024 agniv sarkar

lorem ipsum always made me shrug, but i realize now that i don't know where it's from or what it means because i never learned latin. checking wikipedia it looks like its some kind of alteration of cicero's philosophy, and he's talking about the pursuit of pain and says "i don't like exercising" lol.

but like, how did it become so popular? it looks like something called Letraset used it, and they were popular because they were the "cool" printers, the guys who would let you be all punk without breaking the bank. so thats why they're big, but choosing lorem ipsum?

from looking online, the only plausible theory right now was it was genuinely random. i'm going to make the educated guess that whoever was doing it didn't necessarily know latin and just randomly picked bits and pieces from cicero that were nonsensical. and that's how we got lorem ipsum.
